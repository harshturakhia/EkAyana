About ekAyana

EkAyana is the best way to keep clients engaged with the service as they are on the move.The main aim of this proposal is to develop a meeting management system. EkAyana is a web-based application that provides a platform to organizations for managing and scheduling meetings, and events, displaying agendas, document sharing, etc. The system can be accessed throughout the organization based on credentials provided to users. The system will monitor meetings, plan meetings under constraints expressed by the participants, reschedule meetings based on constraints, support conflict resolutions, and manage all the interactions among participants.
